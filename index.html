About Us

Welcome to Energo, your partner in propelling the future of energy forward. With a passion for sustainability and a commitment to innovation, we are dedicated to transforming the commercial and industrial landscape in the Philippines through cutting-edge solar Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs).

Mission / Vision

Our Mission

At Energo, our mission is clear: to empower businesses across sectors with accessible and sustainable solar energy solutions. We're driven by the belief that clean energy isn't just a choice, but a responsibility we all share in building a better world.

Our Vision

Our vision is rooted in a belief that every roof has the potential to be a source of clean energy. We envision a Philippines where solar power is seamlessly integrated into the fabric of commerce and industry, creating a brighter, more resilient, and sustainable nation.
